Who We Are

A Trusted Bridge Between Talent and Opportunity

Founded in Pokhara, Strive Agency was built on a simple belief: skilled Nepali professionals deserve transparent, ethical pathways to global careers. We partner with licensed European recruitment agencies to source, screen, and place candidates in roles that match their qualifications and ambitions.

Every placement we facilitate follows government-approved (DOFE) channels, ensuring full legal compliance and genuine career development for the people we serve.

How It Works

Our Process

  1. 1

    Initial Consultation

    We meet with candidates or employer partners to understand goals, requirements, and timelines in full detail.

  2. 2

    Profile & Role Matching

    Using our European partner network, we match verified candidates to open positions that align with their skills and aspirations.

    After a successful placement, we maintain contact with both parties to support long-term satisfaction and continued partnership.
